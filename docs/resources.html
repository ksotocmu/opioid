Get Treatment in PA

If you or someone you know are suffering from a substance use disorder, the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania can help. They maintain a confidential hotline 1-800-662-HELP (4357) for information about treatment resources. The hotline is staffed by trained professionals 24 hours a day, seven days a week, and is available in both English and Spanish.

Additionally, a directory of local treatment programs can be found at the link below.

Get Naloxone (Narcan®) in PA

Naloxone, also branded as Narcan®, is a medication that can reverse an overdose caused by an opioid (such as prescription pain medication or heroin). When given during an overdose, naloxone blocks the effects of opioids on the brain and restores breathing within two to eight minutes. Naloxone is safe to use and has no potential for abuse.

Naloxone can be obtained via prescription or by using the standing order for the general public.
